Graham Wafer Pie

Crust - list of ingredients and instructions:

  • 1 and 1/4 cups McCormicks Grahams, rolled fine
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  1. Mix all ingredients throughly.
  2. Put aside one quarter cup of mixture for sprinkling top of pie.
  3. Line a 9 inch pie plate with mixture.

Filling - List of ingredients and instructions:

  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 5 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 and 1/2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • Pinch of salt
  1. Using a double boiler mix the milk, egg yolks, 5 tablespoons of sugar and cornstarch.
  2. Cook until mixture begins to thicken, stirring constantly to keep it free from lumps.
  3. When ready pour over crust.

Meringue - List of ingredients and instructions:

  • 3 egg whites
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  1. Beat egg whites until very stiff.
  2. Gradually add the 3 tablespoons of sugar.
  3. Spread over top of pie creating peaks.
  4. Sprinkle the 1/4 cup graham mixture which was set aside during crust creation.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees until ight brown, approximately 10 to 15 minutes.

Pumpkin Pie

List of ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups pumpkin
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon cloves
  • 3 slightly beaten eggs
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla

How to put it together:

  1. Combine all ingredients and mix until well blended.
  2. Pour mixture into 10" pie plate with made from scratch or pre-made pie crust.
  3. Bake at 450 degrees for 10 minutes.
  4. Reduce heat to 325 and bake for 45 minutes.
  5. To confirm pie is baked completely poke a toothpick into the middle of mixture. When toothpick is removed clean pie is ready.

Carmelitas - Caramel Chocolate Oatmeal Bars

List of ingredients:

  • 1 and 3/4 cups flour
  • 2 cups quick-cooking oats
  • 1 and 1/2 cups brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 14 ounce vanilla caramels, unwrapped
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate pieces
  • 1 cup unsweetened baking chocolate, chopped
  • 1 cup chopped pecans

How to put it together:

  1. Heat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Line a 13x9x2-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving about 1 inch of paper hanging on the sides. Set aside.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, oats, brown sugar, soda, and salt. Stir in the melted butter and mix until crumbly.
  4. Press half of the mixture on the bottom of the prepared pan. Sprinkle with the chopped pecans and chocolate (both the chips and chopped one) over the crust.
  5. In a small saucepan, combine caramels and heavy cream. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly until melted and smooth.
  6. Pour the caramel in the pan, over the chocolate and pecans.
  7. Crumble the reserved oatmeal cookie dough over the caramel layer.
  8. Bake for 23 minutes or until light golden brown.
  9. Cool completely in pan on a wire rack.
    10. Optional:Once cooled, melt 5 to 6 caramels and 1/3 cup chocolate chips and drizzle over the bars.
